About ColorVista!

Welcome to ColorVista! We are passionate about creating a more inclusive and accessible world for individuals with color vision deficiencies. Our app is designed to empower people with colorblindness by providing them with tools to navigate and interact with the colorful world around them more easily.

Mission Statement

At our core, we believe that everyone deserves equal access to information and experiences. Our mission is to break down barriers for individuals with color vision deficiencies, enabling them to fully engage with the world in a way that suits their unique needs.

Our Vision

We envision a world where colorblindness is not a limitation but a difference that is celebrated and accommodated. Through ColorVista, we strive to foster greater understanding and appreciation for the diverse ways in which people perceive color.

Features

ColorVista offers a range of features carefully crafted to assist individuals with color vision deficiencies:

  • Color Blindness Test:

    • Color blindness test makes the user aware about the type of color blindness that they have. This feature will help the user to make decisions about how they want to go on with accessing different features of the app.

  • Color Identification:

    • Easily identify and distinguish between colors with our color detection tool, that gives the HEX value of wherever you point your cursor on the screen.

  • Color Blindness Simulation

    • Upload an image and change the color settings to view how the image would look like from a color-blind person’s perspective. This feature is for people with normal vision to understand how people with various deficiencies see the world around them. This feature will also help the artists who would like to know how a colorblind person would perceive their art.

  • Color correction and representation:

    • Implement color correction algorithms based on user settings. Represent corrected colors through a shift which is based on user’s previous input and tests taken such that a colorblind person may see the image with more clarity

  • Intuitive user interface:

    • Intuitive and users-friendly interface. Initial UI with minimum colors which is suitable for users with all vision deficiencies.

  • Accessibility features for different types of colorblindness:

    • Support for various types of colorblindness (protanopia, deuteranopia, tritanopia)

  • Educational Resources and other Accessibility tools:

    • Learn more about a wider range of other accessibility tools that a color blind person can make use of. We aim to raise awareness and dispel misconceptions surrounding color vision deficiencies.

Join Us in Making a Difference!
